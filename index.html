To Andrea

My oldest friends would think it queer
that I allow my own career
to keep me till such hours awake
so many evenings of the year

I save my changes, then run make
and give my wrists a little shake
The build process fails to complete
it says I’ve made a small mistake.

I search for bugs on every line
Ask my debugger for a sign
When it’s found i’ll feel sublime
‘till then I mutter: “this is fine”
